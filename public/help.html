How to use Fortune Teller

What is Fortune Teller?

Fortune Teller is an app that tracks your daily efforts and good things that happen to you. By recording your efforts, you accumulate "Luck Points" which can influence your fortune!

How do Points Work?

*
Effort (+Points)

When you work hard or achieve something, you earn points. These add up to improve your luck!

*
Good Things (-1 Point)

When something good happens, it means you "used up" some luck. Don't worry - keep working hard!

Features

  • * Home: View your total points and access main functions
  • * Record Effort: Log your hard work and earn points
  • * Good Things: Record lucky moments (uses 1 point)
  • * Fortune: Check your luck based on your points
  • * Graph: See your 7-day point trend
  • * History: Review all your records

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally in your browser. This means:

  • Your data is private and stays on your device
  • No account or login required
  • Data persists even after closing the browser
  • Clearing browser data will reset the app

Tips for Success

  • Record your efforts daily for the best results
  • Be honest about your achievements
  • Check your fortune each morning for motivation
  • Review the graph weekly to see your progress
